Ferrari is a famous Italian car manufacturer founded by Enzo Ferrari in 1939 as Auto Avio Costruzioni. The brand officially became Ferrari in 1947. Based in Maranello, Ferrari is renowned for its luxury sports and racing cars.

Scuderia Ferrari

Scuderia Ferrari, founded by Enzo Ferrari in 1929, it is the most historic and titled team in Formula 1. Its races are characterized by a combination of high technology, exceptional performances and a deep emotional connection with the fans. Ferrari has participated in every Formula 1 season since its inception in 1950, obtaining numerous world titles for both drivers and manufacturers. He won 15 world titles, world constructors' titles: 16, Grand Prix wins: 240 V.
