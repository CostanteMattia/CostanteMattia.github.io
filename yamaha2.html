The hybrid technology used, called HY-KERS, constitutes the perfect balance between maximizing performance and reducing consumption (only 330 g/km of CO2). For the first time a road car from the Maranello company is equipped with dynamics controls integrated with the active aerodynamics and the HY-KERS system. The layout of the interiors is also projected into the future, with a man-machine interface strongly inspired by that of a Formula 1. But above all, the 12-cylinder V-shaped 6,262 cm3 engine is the most powerful naturally aspirated engine ever used on a road Ferrari. The maximum power is 800 HP and the maximum speed reaches 9,250 rpm, guaranteeing extraordinary performance, driving fun and an unmistakable sound. These unprecedented results are due to the work carried out on volumetric, mechanical and combustion efficiency.
